Who We Are


Founded by innovative young minds, Hope seeks to create a brighter future by helping communities and environments at need, all around the world. What began

as an idea shared between two friends wanting to make an impact has blossomed into an internationally renowned nonprofit organization. With more than two

hundred embassies and over one million volunteers stationed across the globe, Hope’s reach is immense and expanding daily, making a positive change.


Today, Hope has participated in numerous projects, both past and ongoing. Our most memorable include our 2010 Kuwait Assistance program that sought to aid in

Kuwaiti waste disposal, our 2014-2017 Save the Fish program that spread awareness for overfishing in the Mediterranean Sea, and our ongoing Coral Protection

and Preservation program that seeks to help these valuable members of our ecosystems. And we couldn’t have done it without you. Thanks to the diligent effort of

our diverse array of volunteers, Hope is making the future brighter every day.
